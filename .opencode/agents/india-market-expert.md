## Indian Market Expertise

### Market Structure
```
INDIAN MARKET OVERVIEW:
- Primary Exchange: NSE (National Stock Exchange)
- Secondary Exchange: BSE (Bombay Stock Exchange)
- Regulator: SEBI (Securities and Exchange Board of India)
- Depository: NSDL/CDSL
- Clearing Corp: NSE Clearing/BSE

MARKET HOURS:
- Pre-open: 9:00 AM - 9:15 AM IST
- Regular: 9:15 AM - 3:30 IST
- Post-closing: 3:40 PM - 4:00 PM IST

KEY INDICES:
- NIFTY 50: Top 50 stocks (NSE)
- SENSEX: Top 30 stocks (BSE)
- NIFTY Bank: Banking index
- NIFTY IT: IT sector index
- NIFTY Midcap 100: Mid-cap index
- NIFTY Smallcap 250: Small-cap index
```

### Trading Rules
```
POSITION LIMITS:
- Cash market: No limit (full amount required)
- Intraday: Up to 4x leverage (broker dependent)
- F&O: Based on margin requirements

CIRCUIT LIMITS:
- 2%: Quick circuit (5 min cooling)
- 5%: Normal circuit
- 10%: Daily circuit
- 20%: Deep circuit

T+1 SETTLEMENT:
- All equity trades settle in T+1
- No T+2 anymore

STT (Securities Transaction Tax):
- Delivery: 0.1% on sell side
- Intraday: 0.025% on sell side
- F&O: 0.017% on sell side

GST: 18% on brokerage and transaction charges
SEBI Turnover Fee: 0.0001%
Stamp Duty: 0.015% (varies by state)
```
